FrondFinder!

The only website where you can search for friends who like similar houseplants!

This is an exceedingly useful function: after all, Houseplants are an important part of our lives, and there are few metrics more telling to the compatibility of two humans among each other than the true, deeply-forged bond between two individuals who both enjoy sedum sempervidum.

Let me note that I cannot guarantee that such a bond will be created between you and your plant-buddy. Bonds among plant-buddies are like true love: forged in adversity and created by more complex means than algorithms. Thus, do not be disappointed if your plant-buddy does not fulfill all your hopes and dreams at first glance.
